Carrot Academy Hub: Centralizing Shopper Education

Project Overview

Created a hub to streamline educational access, significantly improving the learning experience.

Objective

Increase shopper engagement and streamline access to educational content through a single, accessible hub.

Approach

  • Utilized survey insights to identify shopper preferences and barriers.
  • Developed a hub layout and content strategy focusing on gamification, learning announcements, and access support.
  • Collaborated with cross-functional teams for design, content moderation, and technical structure. Conducted extensive testing to ensure user-friendly navigation and engagement.

Outcome

The launch of the Carrot Academy Hub dramatically transformed the learning experience for Instacart shoppers, leading to a 5x increase in community activity and significantly higher engagement. Previously, monthly content views averaged 2,000; post-launch, this soared to 10,000. Engagement metrics saw remarkable improvements: likes per post increased from 20 to 160, comments rose from an average of 5 to 50, and active participating members per month jumped from 15 to 60. These figures underscore the hub's success in enhancing accessibility and engagement with educational content.
