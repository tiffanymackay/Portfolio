Revitalizing Shopper Connection: A Strategic OKR Approach

Project Overview

In 2023, faced with the challenge of decreased engagement metrics, I launched an innovative approach to revitalize shopper engagement through strategic objectives and key results (OKR).

Objective

To counteract a dip in engagement metrics by implementing a multifaceted engagement strategy, including manual targeted messaging and the introduction of a programmatic Learning Sweepstakes.

Approach

  • Pivoted strategies to optimize existing resources in light of CRM support loss, focusing on manual targeted messaging and a Learning Sweepstakes.
  • Enhanced the Shop Talk Learning Hub content strategy and integrated Typeforms into cross-functional emails.

Outcome

Despite initial setbacks, the Learning Sweepstakes and strategic content interventions in the Shop Talk Learning Hub marked a significant uptick in engagement metrics, seeing month-over-month improvements of 2-4% across our 600,000 shopper base, reflecting the effectiveness of our agile, tailored engagement strategies.

This initiative underscores the importance of resilience and adaptability in engagement strategies, proving that strategic shifts and innovative approaches can lead to positive outcomes even in challenging circumstances.
