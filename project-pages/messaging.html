Strategic Messaging for Shopper Engagement

Project Overview

Aimed to deepen understanding of shopper motivations for lesson engagement at Instacart, driving a strategic overhaul of communication and educational content.

Objective

Identify key motivations behind shoppersâ€™ decisions to engage with lessons, utilizing insights to tailor messaging and increase lesson completion rates.

Approach

  • Conducted a detailed analysis focusing on understanding shopper motivations through surveys and data analysis.
  • Collaborated with User Experience Research (UXR) for survey design and analysis, leveraging AI tools for efficient data pattern identification.
  • Segmented shoppers by tenure, performance, and geographical location to tailor lesson recommendations.

Outcome

The strategic targeting and personalization of lesson communications led to a 5x increase in conversion rates from an average of 2-3% to 10-12% across targeted groups. This initiative underscored the importance of aligning educational content with shopper motivations, significantly enhancing the value and relevance of lessons offered.
