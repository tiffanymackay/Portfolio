About

By day I'm a UX/Web Designer and Jr. Front-end Developer, and by night, I'm a digital artist.

I was born in California but now live in Washington. And on rainy days, you'll most likely find me sitting with my digital tablet creating new digital paintings.

When not exploring the West Coast, I can be found nestled amongst Scotland's isles or riding zebras along the southern shores of Cape Town.

I'm kidding. I don't ride the zebras. But I do sometimes have to share my lunch with a troop of baboons.

Before finishing my Bachelors in Technical Communications with a 4.11 GPA, I was a Chief Stewardess in the yachting industry, sailing along the inlets, straights, and sounds of the US East Coast, across the Atlantic ocean, and through the Mediterranean Sea over the span of six years.
