📖 my story

After graduating with an AA in Business Administration from San Joaquin Delta Community College, I balanced roles as a hairdresser and waitress before diving into the Superyacht industry for six years, traveling to over 20 countries with my husband. In 2018, I pivoted to entrepreneurship in Cape Town, SA, where my passion for web and product design blossomed alongside ghostwriting and editorial work.

Completing my BS in Technical Communications and User Experience, I honed my skills in creative problem-solving, front-end technologies, and user-centric design. In 2020, my husband and I moved back to Sacramento, CA, to focus on our careers, accompanied by our two cats.
