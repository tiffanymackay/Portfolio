📖 my story

After high school, I pursued Business Administration at San Joaquin Delta Community College while juggling roles as a hairdresser and waitress. Earning my AA in 2012, I embarked on global travels after obtaining my passport, venturing into the Superyacht industry for six years alongside my partner. We explored 20+ countries, making places like Sardinia, Italy; Nice, France; Mykonos, Greece; and Palma, Spain our temporary homes.

In 2018, I transitioned from yachting to entrepreneurship, launching multiple businesses and settling in Cape Town, SA with my now-husband. Alongside these ventures, I undertook ghostwriting contracts and editorial roles for a magazine. Amidst this journey, my affinity for designing and developing websites and products flourished, sparking my passion for creative problem-solving.

Completing my BS in Technical Communications further solidified my knack for addressing user-centric challenges. The year 2020 marked a return to California, as my husband and I left Cape Town to prioritize our careers, establishing our base in Sacramento, CA, alongside our two feline companions.
