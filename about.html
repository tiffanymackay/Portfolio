📖 my story

After graduating high school, I studied Business Administration at San Joaquin Delta Community College while I worked as a hairdresser and waitress. After finishing my AA in 2012, I got my passport and left California to travel the world.

Over the next 6 years, I worked in the Superyacht industry and sailed with my boyfriend visiting over 20+ countries and territories, making different countries our homes like Sardinia, Italy; Nice, France; Mykonos, Greece; Palma, Spain.

Then, after leaving the yachting industry in 2018, my (now) husband and I started other businesses and moved to Cape Town, SA. In total, I've started and ran over 6 different businesses in between ghost writing contracts and editing for a magazine.

Through the creation of different small businesses, a love for designing and building websites and products was born. But it wasn’t until going back to finish my Bachelors in Science in Technical Communications that I learned I love solving creative problems for users.

In 2020, my husband and I left Cape Town to move to the Pacific Northwest to focus on our Product Design careers, living in Seattle, WA with our two kitties.
